### How to Come to EURECOM

- The nearest airport is **Nice Côte d'Azur Airport** (approximately 20 km from EURECOM).
- For accommodation, we recommend the coastal cities of **Antibes** and **Juan-les-Pins**, from where public buses are available (30 to 40 mins).

- The conference plans to provide a **chartered bus service** (included in registration, no additional fee) as follows:
    - Mornings (Days 1, 2, 3): Antibes → EURECOM
    - Evenings (Days 1 & 3): EURECOM → Antibes
    - Evening (Day 2): EURECOM → Nice (for the conference dinner)
    - Late evening (Day 2): Nice → Antibes (after dinner)

- The bus will depart each morning from ``Pl. Général de Gaulle'' in Antibes:

- If you plan to use the bus, we therefore recommend staying in **Antibes** or **Juan-les-Pins**, close to the departure point.

-  **Nice** is another good option, but buses may take longer.
